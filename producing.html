Producing Portfolio — After The Whistle

Alongside my on-air work, I serve as Executive Producer of After The Whistle, a weekly live sports show covering Penn State and Central Pennsylvania. Each episode is pitched, shot, edited, written, and aired in under six hours.

I lead editorial pitch meetings, manage the rundown, coordinate reporters and anchors, and oversee editing and graphics to make sure the show hits air on time and feels professional.

  • Lead weekly editorial meetings and approve segments
  • Build and code rundowns; create/coordinate graphics in Ross Inception
  • Edit highlights, VOs, and packages in Adobe Premiere
  • Direct live show execution and timing
  • Manage and mentor 100+ student journalists
