About Owen

I'm a sports broadcaster who focuses on clear, descriptive storytelling — helping viewers understand not just what happened, but why it matters. My goal is always to bring energy, context, and narrative to the games I cover, whether I'm anchoring in studio, reporting live, or breaking down plays as an analyst.

Alongside my on-air work, I've developed strong producing experience as Executive Producer of After The Whistle, a weekly live sports show created from scratch in a single night. Producing has strengthened my editorial judgment and given me a deeper understanding of what talent needs to succeed on camera.

That combination of broadcasting and producing experience gives me a unique perspective in sports media: the on-air presence of a broadcaster, and the behind-the-scenes awareness of a producer.
